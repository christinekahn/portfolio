Christine K — Music

Classically trained coloratura soprano based in Portland, OR. Raised in the Philippines on choir and musical theatre, she found electronic music while working at Native Instruments in Hollywood and now records from her home studio — operatic, sensual vocals over electronic production, somewhere between trip-hop and classical.
